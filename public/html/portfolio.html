Word Guess Game

To start the game you must press any key on the keyboard (the first press will not be counted as a right or wrong press). After that a line will appear on the screen with the number of letters in the word. You must guess the last name of the person.

During the game, you will have some hints (from the left of the screen) that will change when you press the letters. the first hint is the person's profession (appears when first pressed), the second one is the middle name of the person (appears after the third incorrect letter), the third one is film or song with the participation of the person (appears after the third incorrect letter), the last one is the first name of the person (appears after the ninth incorrect letter), be careful after this, you will have only one attempt to guess the person.

Star War RPG

Few books or games have had as enduring an impact upon the Star Wars galaxy and its fans as Star Wars™: The Roleplaying Game. Originally published by West End Games in 1987, it arrived at a time when the future of the Star Wars galaxy was uncertain, and it captivated a whole generation of gamers with rules and guidelines that made it possible to design and enjoy adventures truly worthy of the Star Wars universe and its ongoing space opera.

You must select a character from the left side of the screen, after that this character will fight for you.

You must select a character on the right side of the screen, after that this character will be your enemy

Clicking on the "ATTACK" button you start the game.

Around The World

In this game, you will find interesting facts about our world, for example, the longest river, the largest tree, etc., expand your knowledge about it.

You must press the button start for starting the game.

In the top of the page, you can see your time (you have only 25 seconds for each question).

Under of the time you see the question.

On the bottom, you see 4 answers. One of them is correct.

Soccer Players

The project was created by the GIPHY API. The limit was 10 pictures.

When page uploaded you'll see the list with names of several soccer players.

You must press on one of that player name.

After that, you will see photos of the player By clicking on the picture, it will become animated. If you press again in the same picture it is become not animated.

If your favorite player is not in the list of players, you can add him by writing his name in the "Add Player" chapter and click on the button "Add Player". Then follow the instructions in chapter 2.
